Sri Guru Harkrishan Sen. Sec. Public School, G.T. Road, Amritsar, a front-runner in the chain of institutions is the first school in the region to be affiliated with CBSE. Founded in the name of Sri Guru Harkrishan Sahib Ji, the eighth guru of the Sikhs, the school was established in 1967 under the flagship of Chief Khalsa Diwan.
